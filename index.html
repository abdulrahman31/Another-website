Rolling updates on coronavirus
(COVID-19)

The National Institute for Public Health and the Environment
(RIVM) is providing advice and information on the new coronavirus.
The organisation provides advice to doctors,
laboratories and municipal health services (GGDs) on tackling the virus in the Netherlands.
You can find detailed information on the new coronavirus on the RIVM website.
